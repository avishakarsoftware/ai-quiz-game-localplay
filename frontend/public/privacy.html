Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

Revelry Quiz ("we", "our", "the app") is a multiplayer quiz game created by Revelry (revelryapp.me). Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Revelry Quiz collects minimal data to operate the game:

  • Nickname and avatar — chosen by you when joining a game. These are temporary and not stored after the game session ends.
  • Quiz topics — the topics you type to generate quizzes. These are sent to an AI service (Google Gemini) to create questions and are not stored permanently.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising

Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini to generate quiz questions from your chosen topics. Your quiz topic text is sent to the Gemini API for this purpose. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data.

Data Retention

All game data (rooms, players, scores, quizzes) is stored temporarily in server memory and automatically deleted after the game session ends or after 30 minutes of inactivity. We do not maintain a database of user information.

Children's Privacy

Revelry Quiz does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to play.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@revelryapp.me.
